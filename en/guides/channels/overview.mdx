A **Channel** is the bridge between the AI Agent and the messaging platform used by the customer. Timely.ai supports multiple simultaneous channels — all Conversations arrive centralized in the Inbox, regardless of origin.

## Overview

Timely.ai offers five channel types, each with its own configuration characteristics and features.

### Key features

- Unified Inbox with Conversations from all channels in a single queue
- Channel filters in the Inbox to segment support interactions
- Media support (image, audio, video, and document) across all channels
- A single Agent can be connected to multiple channels at the same time
- Complete Chat History for each Conversation preserved regardless of the channel of origin
