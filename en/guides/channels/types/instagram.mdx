---
title: "Instagram"
description: "Connect your Instagram Business account and handle DMs automatically via Meta OAuth."
---

The Instagram channel lets your Agent handle Instagram **Direct Messages** in real time. The connection uses Meta's OAuth flow and requires an Instagram Business or Creator account linked to a Facebook Page.

## Connection flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Channels in the Dashboard">
    In the sidebar, click **Channels** and then **Add channel**. Select **Instagram**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect with Instagram">
    A Meta popup opens. Make sure popups are enabled in your browser before clicking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and select accounts">
    Inside the popup, log in with the Facebook account that manages the Page linked to Instagram. Select the Facebook Page and the corresponding Instagram account. Grant all requested permissions — especially `instagram_manage_messages` and `pages_messaging`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for confirmation">
    After authorizing, the popup closes and Timely.ai verifies the status in the background. When the connection is established, the badge changes to **Connected** and the username `@youraccount` appears on screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link to the Agent">
    Confirm that the correct Agent is selected for this Channel in **Agents → [agent] → Channels**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test message receiving">
    Send a DM to the connected Instagram account from another profile. The message should appear in the Inbox within a few seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
