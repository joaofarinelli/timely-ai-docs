The **Inbox** is the operational hub of Timely.ai. All active Conversations — from WhatsApp, Instagram, Web Widget, Telegram, and Slack — arrive in a single place, organized and ready for handling.

## Main structure

| Section | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|
| **Conversation list** | Displays filtered threads with contact name, source channel, time since last message, and current status |
| **Active chat** | Complete Chat History of the selected Conversation, reply field, and actions (close, transfer, notes) |
| **Context panel** | Contact data, assigned Tags, custom fields, and previous activity |

## Quick navigation

- **All** tab — Conversations visible according to the user's Role in the Workspace
- **Mine** tab — only threads assigned to the logged-in user
- **Unassigned** tab — Conversations without a responsible party, available to claim
- Combined filters by channel, Agent, attendant, Tag, and status
