title: "Roles and Permissions"
description: "Complete Role matrix in Timely.ai and what each level can do within the Inbox and the Workspace."
---

Timely.ai's access control is based on Roles assigned per Workspace. Each member receives a single Role that determines what they can view and perform in the Inbox and other areas of the platform.

## Available Roles

| Role | Access level | Description |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| `owner` | Unrestricted | Full Workspace access, including billing, data deletion, and ownership transfer |
| `manager` | High | Visibility of all Conversations, Team creation, SLA reports, and member invitations |
| `trainer` | Intermediate | `attendant` support capabilities plus Agent editing and review of all Conversations for audit purposes |
| `attendant` | Operational | Front-line support on Conversations assigned to their Team; no access to settings or reports |
| `member` | Minimal | View explicitly assigned Conversations; no action capability |

## Role behavior in the Inbox

| Action | owner / manager | trainer | attendant | member |
|------|:--------------:|:-------:|:---------:|:------:|
| View all Workspace Conversations | Yes | Yes | — | — |
| View unassigned Team Conversations | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Claim Conversation (self-assign) | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Activate Takeover (pause AI) | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Transfer Conversation | Yes | Yes | Yes | — |
| Assign Conversation to another member | Yes | — | — | — |
| Create and edit Teams | Yes | — | — | — |
| View SLA reports | Yes | — | — | — |

## Operational Role: attendant

The `attendant` has scope restricted to their Team and assigned Conversations:

- Views Conversations assigned to them and unassigned Conversations from the Team(s) they belong to.
- Can claim, reply, transfer, close, and add Notes on Conversations within their scope.

## Administrative Roles in the Inbox

Managers and owners have global visibility and control over Teams and members:

- Access and interact with any Workspace Conversation, regardless of assignment.

> Owners are the only ones with `manage_billing`. Managers have `manage_team`, `view_reports`, `invite_users`, and `manage_company` — enough to operate the Workspace without needing billing access.

## Usage examples

| Scenario | Ideal Role |
|---------|-----------|
| Support attendant responding to customers on WhatsApp | `attendant` |
| Quality analyst reviewing Conversations and adjusting prompts | `trainer` |
| Team manager creating Teams and monitoring SLA | `manager` |
| Founder with access to plan and billing | `owner` |
| External stakeholder following a specific case | `member` |
