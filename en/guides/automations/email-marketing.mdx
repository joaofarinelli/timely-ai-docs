The Timely.ai Email Marketing module covers three distinct scenarios: **manual campaigns** for one-time communication, **lifecycle emails** for automatic onboarding and engagement, and the **weekly digest** with a weekly activity summary. All sends go through Brevo (formerly Sendinblue).

## Email campaigns

Campaigns are one-time sends — such as product announcements, newsletters, or notices. You select a template, define the recipient list, and schedule or send immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Email Marketing">
    In the sidebar, go to **Automations > Email Marketing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new campaign">
    Click **New Campaign**. Fill in the subject, sender name, and reply-to address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the template">
    Select an existing template or write the content directly. Variables in the format `{{name}}` are substituted for each recipient.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define the list">
    Import contacts via CSV, select CRM segments, or choose manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send or schedule">
    Send immediately or schedule for a specific date and time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lifecycle emails

Lifecycle emails are automatically triggered by `lifecycle-email-processor` based on milestones in the user journey. The `email-scheduler` runs via cron (recommended: every hour) and identifies eligible users.

| Email | When it is sent | Condition |
|---|---|---|
| **Welcome** | Shortly after signup | User created an account |
| **Onboarding D+1** | 24h after signup | User has not yet completed onboarding |
| **Activation milestone** | When the user reaches a configured milestone | Configurable per account |

<Note>
  Each lifecycle email is sent at most once per user. The system checks the `email_logs` table to see if that `template_slug` has already been sent before triggering again.
</Note>

## Weekly Digest

The `lifecycle-weekly-digest` automatically generates and sends a weekly summary. The digest includes:

- Conversations handled during the week
- Agent performance metrics
- Highlights of new contacts and leads
- Comparison with the previous week

The send occurs every Monday morning for users who have not opted out.

## Preferences and opt-out

Users can manage their email preferences by accessing the preference center via the link in the footer of each email. Timely.ai respects opt-outs immediately — users who have unsubscribed will not receive any more marketing emails.

<Warning>
  Transactional emails (e.g., password reset, account confirmation) are always sent regardless of marketing preferences.
</Warning>

## Performance metrics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Open rate" icon="envelope-open">
    Percentage of recipients who opened the email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Click rate" icon="arrow-pointer">
    Percentage who clicked on at least one link.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bounce rate" icon="ban">
    Emails that could not be delivered (bounce).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Unsubscribes" icon="user-minus">
    Users who opted out after receiving the email.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Configure an authenticated sending domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) in Brevo to maximize deliverability. Emails sent from unauthenticated domains are more likely to land in spam.
</Tip>
