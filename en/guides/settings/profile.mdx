Profile settings let you personalize your personal information within the platform, update your photo, and manage your account security. Profile settings are individual — each team member has their own profile.

## Accessing profile settings

In the sidebar, click **Settings**. The **Profile** tab is the default view of the settings section.

## Profile data

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Full name">
    Your name as it appears to other team members in the members list and activity logs. It is not shown to end customers in service conversations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email">
    The email is the primary identifier of your account. To change the email, contact support — it cannot be edited directly in the interface for security reasons.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Phone">
    Optional field. May be used by the Timely.ai support team for contact.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Job title">
    Free text to identify your role in the company (e.g., "Customer Service Manager", "Developer"). Visible only internally to team members.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Industry">
    Select the company's industry from the available fields: health & wellness, beauty, education, technology, consulting, legal, financial, or other. This information is used to calibrate the BI Dashboard benchmark.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Updating your profile photo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the current photo or camera icon">
    In the profile section, click the avatar area to open the file selector.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select an image">
    Choose an image from your device. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, and WebP. Maximum size: 5 MB.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the upload">
    The image is uploaded and processed automatically. The avatar is updated in the interface as soon as the upload finishes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To remove the current photo without replacing it, click the trash icon that appears when hovering over the avatar.

## Saving changes

After editing the desired fields, click **Save changes** at the bottom of the form. The button is enabled only when there are differences from the original data. A confirmation toast appears when the changes are saved successfully.

## Account security

The security section is on the same settings page, below the profile data.

### Changing the password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter the current password">
    Type your current password in the **Current password** field to confirm your identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the new password">
    Type the new password in the **New password** field. Minimum requirements: 8 characters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the new password">
    Repeat the new password in the **Confirm new password** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Change password**. The password is updated immediately and you remain logged in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  After changing the password, sessions open on other devices are not automatically terminated. If you suspect unauthorized access, change the password and contact support.
</Warning>

## Notifications

Notification preferences are in the **Notifications** tab within Settings. You can configure:

- Alerts for new conversations assigned to you
- Daily email summaries
- BI Dashboard budget alerts (when applicable to your role)

Notifications are individual — each member configures their own preferences independently.
